a quick guide

how it works

The streaming platform reads directly from the same files you upload here. When you upload a track and edit its metadata, those changes are reflected in the streaming app within seconds — there's no separate publishing step.

uploading music

Organize your music into albums. Each album can have a cover image, genre, and year. Upload tracks one at a time — MP3, FLAC, M4A, and OGG are all supported.

metadata

Metadata is the information embedded in your audio file — title, artist, track number, genre, composer, ISRC, BPM. The streaming platform reads these tags directly from your files, so keeping them accurate matters. After uploading a track, use the "edit metadata" link to review and correct the tags.
